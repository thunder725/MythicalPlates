=-=-=-=-= SummoningModule.cs =-=-=-=-= 
- IS A MODULE SCRIPT
- Parent script for AllmightySinnoh.cs and IndividualPlateModule.cs
- Receives information (Strikes, Solve, Logging) from PlateBase and handles it.
- Transmits Bomb Info (KMBombModule, KMBombInfo, KMAudio, etc.) to the PlateBase

Children:
> AllmightySinnoh.cs		(module Allmighty Sinnoh)
> IndividualPlateModule.cs	(all 18 other Modules in one .cs, configured on the Prefab)


ReceiveStrike() and ReceiveSolve() are to be created in Children, and to be called by PlateBases

PlateBases can call (and SHOULD) summoningModule.ModuleLog(moduleId, "message {0}", args)


=-=-=-=-= PlateBase.cs =-=-=-=-= 
- NOT A MODULE SCRIPT
- Contained on the Plate itself
- Base for the 18 individual Plates so they have a common pool of Methods and Variables
- Designed to be summoned by either its own Module, or Allmighty Sinnoh.
- Reports information (Strikes, Solve, Logging, etc.) back to the SummoningModule, not to the bomb directly
- Receives Bomb Info (KMBombModule, KMBombInfo, KMAudio, etc.) from the SummoningModule

Children:
> BlankPlate.cs
> FistPlate.cs
> SkyPlate.cs
> ToxicPlate.cs
> EarthPlate.cs
> StonePlate.cs
> InsectPlate.cs
> SpookyPlate.cs
